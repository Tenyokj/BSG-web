How to Donate with MetaMask

Follow these simple steps to send cryptocurrency using MetaMask browser extension.

1

Install MetaMask

If you don't have MetaMask installed, download it from the official website and set up your wallet. It's a browser extension available for Chrome, Firefox, Brave, and Edge.

2

Copy Wallet Address

Click the "Copy ETH Address" button above to copy our Ethereum wallet address to your clipboard.

3

Open MetaMask

Click the MetaMask icon in your browser toolbar and unlock your wallet with your password.

4

Send ETH or Tokens

Click "Send" in MetaMask, paste our wallet address, enter the amount you wish to donate, and confirm the transaction.

5

Wait for Confirmation

Your donation will be processed on the Ethereum blockchain. It usually takes a few minutes to confirm.
